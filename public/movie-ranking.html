The Best Movies According to Megan

My top 3 movies of all time.

  1. Ladybird

    2. A coming of age tale set in Sacramento, CA. Fraught mother-daughter relationship, friendship, romance, and theater.

  2. The Sound of Music

    3. Julie Andrews plays a wannabe nun with ADHD who becomes a nanny.

  3. What We Do in the Shadows

    4. A household of vampires tries to stay under the radar in New Zealand, hilarity ensues.
